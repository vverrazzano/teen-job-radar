Teen Job Radar: Smart First Jobs in Porter County

1. Safe Employer Checklist
  • Background checks required
  • Mentorship or training offered
  • Clear wage and schedule policies
  • Drug-free workplace policy posted
  • Supervisors with youth experience

5. Interview Prep & Gut Check

Questions to Ask

  • “What kind of training do you offer?”
  • “Who will I report to, and how often do we check in?”
  • “Is there a drug-free workplace policy?”

Red Flags

  • Vague answers about safety or supervision
  • Pressure to work off the books
  • Inconsistent scheduling or pay structure

Gut Check Tips

  • Trust your instincts
  • Talk it through with a parent or mentor
  • You’re allowed to say no—even after accepting an offer
